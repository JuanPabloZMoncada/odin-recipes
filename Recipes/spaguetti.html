Spaguetti - Recipe

In this recipe we will learn to make spaguetti with very simple ingredientes.

List of ingridientes:

  • 250 grams of spaguetti
  • 1 cup of table cream
  • 150 grams of Bacon
  • Some corriander
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. Heat a pan with some water, when it start to boil, throw the spaguetti pasta in there with some salt.
  2. Past 15 minutes, dry the pasta.
  3. Heat another pan, an throw the bacon with some oil.
  4. When the bacon is done, put the table cream into the pan and when it is hot enough throw in there the pasta.
  5. let it heat it up a little bit and in the end you can add some corriander.
